Dee Briggs

Dee Briggs was born in 1968 in Burgettstown, Pennsylvania. She received a Masters of Architecture degree from Yale University in 2002 with undergraduate studies in architecture from the City College of New York. Briggs began making art in 2002, exhibits nationally and has taught in the schools of art and architecture at Carnegie Mellon University. She lives and works in New York and Pittsburgh.
