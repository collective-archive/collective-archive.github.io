Ryan Lammie

Ryan Lammie was born in Pittsburgh and completed his BFA in 2012 at the Pratt Institute, Brooklyn, New York. His practice incorporates mixed-media and found objects. He is active in the Pittsburgh arts community in his role as founder/director of Radiant Hall.
