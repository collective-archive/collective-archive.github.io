Ellis Gallery at Carnegie Mellon University

Located within the School of Art, the Ellis Gallery is available for students to show work throughout the year. From the website: "Students are encouraged to write proposals to present their work in these venues to fellow classmates, the university community and the public at-large. Students are also encouraged to pursue showing their work in exhibition and non-traditional venues around Pittsburgh, to further their creative connections with the city and expose their work to broader audiences."
