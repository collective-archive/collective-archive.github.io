Kyle Milne

Kyle Milne was born in Port Colborne, Ontario and has lived and worked in Pittsburgh for the last 25 years. In 2009 he completed his MFA at Indiana University of Pennsylvania. Milne has exhibited locally, nationally, and internationally with solo and group shows in Slovenia, Croatia, Pennsylvania, Oregon, and Virginia. Through sculpture and installation, his minimalist-based works are influenced by his travels and the concepts that form within the journey. Interactions with new cultures and the exploration of identity drive his creative process to transform commonplace materials into works that engage the viewer.
