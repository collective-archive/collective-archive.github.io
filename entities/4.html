Radiant Hall

Radiant hall is an artist-run, studio complex located in a former Polish social hall in the Pittsburgh neighborhood of Lawrenceville. It has at various times been a professional home to artists including Terry Boyd, Meghan Olson, Seth Clark, Gavin Benjamin and others.
