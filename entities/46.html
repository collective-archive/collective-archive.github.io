Travis Rohrbaugh

Since graduating from Rhode Island School of Design in 2008, Rohrbaugh has lived in Pittsburgh teaching glass classes while working to establish himself as an artist working in glass. His large-scale collaborative glass installation, “Thaw,” representing a life size woolly mammoth skeleton, was on display last summer at Phipps Conservatory. He currently works as a studio technician at the Pittsburgh Glass Center.
