Preserving Pittsburgh's Art Scene—For All

Collective Archive documents the people, places, and work in Pittsburgh’s art scene. We focus on small and emerging artists, organizations, and spaces so we can tell a complete story of the art scene at any point in time.

Technology

The Archive is built on Collective Access, an open-source, cloud-based software for managing and publishing museum and archival collections.
