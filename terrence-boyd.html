Terrence Boyd

Terry Boyd attended Carnegie Mellon University, completing a BFA in Painting / Contextual Practice (2009) and a Masters in Arts Management (2013). His multi-disciplinary practice includes painting (abstract and figurative), performance, drawing, sewing. He has won numerous awards for his work including a nomination in 2014 for Pittsburgh Emerging Artist of the Year.
