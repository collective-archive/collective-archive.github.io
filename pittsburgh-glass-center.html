Pittsburgh Glass Center

Kathleen Mulcahy and Ron Desmett co-founded the Pittsburgh Glass Center in 2001, with the support of the Urban Redevelopment Authority and William Block. It promotes the art of glassmaking through exhibitions, classes, residencies and by providing access to glassmaking facilities and studios.
