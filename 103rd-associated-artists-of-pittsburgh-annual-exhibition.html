103rd Associated Artists of Pittsburgh Annual Exhibition

From the exhibition website: "This exhibition is the oldest and longest continuing survey of regional art in the country. ...After the 2013 exhibition, Barbara Jones, Chief Curator of the Westmoreland Museum of American Art approached AAP offering to host the Annual Exhibition at the Westmoreland Museum of American Art @rt 30, the Museum's temporary site while the main building is undergoing a major renovation and expansion. Barbara Jones is serving as juror for this exhibition which often draws as many as 250 artists. Jurying took place on April 28, 2014 and was open to all artists within a 150-mile radius of Pittsburgh. Artists do not have to be current members of the AAP."
